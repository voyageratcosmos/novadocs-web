PRIVACY POLICY FOR NOVADOCS AI

Effective Date: June 30, 2026

At NovaDocs AI, we hold data privacy as our highest developer tenet. This policy explains how we process files:

1. Information Processing: We do NOT run document servers. The pictures snapped by your camera run OCR locally. Structured analysis is processed by stateless Google Gemini APIs. Scans never stay on our servers.

2. Google Drive Permissions: The app utilizes the file-scope storage permission drive.file. This ensures NovaDocs AI cannot access, view, or modify existing folders on your Drive, keeping other files completely confidential.

3. Device Permissions: We request CAMERA permissions for physical document captures and INTERNET access to process sync and API tags.
